2. What Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways:

Information You Provide

  • Discord sign-in: When you sign in with Discord, we receive your Discord user ID and profile information you choose to share.
  • Purchases: When you purchase a subscription or product, we collect your email and order details to complete the transaction. Stripe, our payment processor, handles your payment details securely—we do not store full credit card numbers.
  • Support: If you contact us for support, we collect the messages and information you choose to share.

Information From Your Use of the Services

  • MDT/CAD data: Data you enter into the MDT/CAD system, such as department names, dispatch records, reports, and tickets.
  • Logs and diagnostics: IP address, device type, and browser information, which help us keep the Services secure and reliable.
  • Essential cookies: Cookies that enable logins, secure sessions, and payment processing. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking outside of the Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Process payments and fulfill your orders.
  • Authenticate logins and manage accounts.
  • Communicate with you about your account, purchases, and updates.
  • Monitor for security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only when necessary, including with:

  • Stripe for payments and fraud prevention.
  • Supabase for database and authentication services.
  • Google Cloud for hosting payment infrastructure.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) for custom domain hosting.
  • Sentry for error monitoring.
  • Resend for transactional email delivery.
  • Discord for authentication and community integration.
  • Roblox APIs to verify linked accounts and purchases.

We may also share information if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data Retention

  • Order and payment records are retained as required by financial and tax regulations.
  • MDT/CAD data is retained until you delete it or your account/subscription ends.
  • Log and diagnostic data are kept for a limited period to maintain security and resolve issues.

6. Security of Your Information

We use safeguards such as HTTPS, secure hosting, access controls, and modern encryption practices to protect your data. While no system is completely secure, we are committed to maintaining strong protections.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have specific rights:

  • EU and UK (GDPR/UK GDPR): Access, correction, deletion, portability, objection, restriction, and withdrawal of consent.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know, access, correct, and delete personal information. We do not sell or share personal information.
  • Canada (PIPEDA): Right to access, correct, and withdraw consent subject to legal requirements.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at jose.moranurena@jmusolutionsllc.com.
